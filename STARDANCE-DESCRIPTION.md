# Stardance application description (~960 characters)

ChaoSeed is a browser-based true random number generator that turns physical chaos into
certified randomness. Point your webcam at anything chaotic - your hand, water, smoke, a
lamp, or just the sensor's own noise - and ChaoSeed harvests the unpredictable motion frame
by frame, conditions it with SHA-3, and mixes it with your operating system's RNG so the
output is never weaker than the system. It doesn't just claim to be random: it proves it
live, with an entropy meter and NIST SP 800-22 statistical tests you can watch in real time.
Then you actually use the randomness - generate passwords, keys, dice rolls, or download raw
random bytes. Inspired by Cloudflare's wall of lava lamps, ChaoSeed shrinks that idea into
one open-source web app that is part tool, part live demonstration of how true randomness is
really born from the physical world. Educational, transparent and genuinely usable - no
installs, just open it in your browser.
